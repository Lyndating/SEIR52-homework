Yukihiro Matsumoto

Biography Hometown Trivia

Yukihiro Matsumoto, a.k.a Matz is a renowned Japanese computer scientist and professional programmer famously known for creating the programming language Ruby.

He is the the Chief Architect of Ruby at an online cloud platform-as-a-service called Heroku in San Franscisco. He is a fellow of Rakuten Institute of Technology, a research and development organisation in Rakuten Inc. He was appointed to the role of technical advisor for VASILY, Inc. starting in June 2014.

He is the head of research at a company called Network Communications Laborartory, which is an open source systems integrator.
