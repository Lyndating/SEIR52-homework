Yukihiro Matsumoto

Biography Hometown Trivia

Matsumoto was born in Tottori Prefecture in Japan, currently living in Shimane-ken with his wife and family.

Growing up, Matz taught himself the complex skill of programming up until high school. When he went on to university, he continued this path, specialising in computer science. During university he became involved with research departments that were working on programming languages and compilers.

He went on to graduate from the Univeristy of Tsukaba with a degree in Information Science.
