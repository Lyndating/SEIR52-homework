About Me

I am a Hungarian-American mathematician, physicist, computer scientist, engineer and polymath. I am regarded as perhaps the mathematician with the widest coverage of the subject in my time and is said to have been "the last representative of the great mathematicians who were equally at home in pure and applied mathematics".I integrated pure and applied sciences.
