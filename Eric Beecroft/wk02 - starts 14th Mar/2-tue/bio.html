Jenn Schiffer's Impressive Bio

Jenn Schiffer is a code and content creator based in Jersey City. Jenn earned both a Bachelors and Masters in Computer Science from Montclair State University, then joining the department to work on undergraduate and graduate level curriculum, accreditation, and advising of hundreds of students.

A proponent of open web technology and ethical data science, she left academia to work in the web development industry, with stops as senior engineer at the National Basketball Association and as open web engineer at Bocoup. She's built software that runs the gamut from sports stats delivery and healthcare price crowdsourcing, to databases helping doctors find the best cancer treatment for their patients.

Her current role is Director of Community at Glitch, where she's responsible for supporting, growing and advocating for the friendliest community of coders. She spends lots of time cultivating

She has spoken all over the world at nearly 100 events about JavaScript, ethics, and art. Jenn has organized the developer meetup JerseyScript and was a founding core team member of the current iteration of Code For Jersey City. She's an Emerging Philanthropist at the Women's Prison Association.

You probably know Jenn Schiffer from her side hustle - make8bitart.com

You probably didn't know that she has a perfect 5.0 passenger rating on Uber and a screenshot to prove it!
